RaidSplitter - WoW Addon

PoC addon for extensive raid chat controls

An addon designed to enhance raid communication by allowing raid leaders and assists to send party-specific raid warnings within a raid.

This addon gives leaders the ability to target individual parties (1-8) with tailored warnings, or to send messages to all parties except the specified ones.

With a simple and intuitive interface, it lists all parties and their members, ensuring effective coordination during complex raids. Using the whisper system, this addon ensures that only the designated recipients receive the targeted messages.

RPCombat - WoW Addon

PoC Addon for Turn-Based Combat

RPCombat is an attempt at providing a clean, easy & intuitive interface for organizing combat in RP.

Party/Raid leaders can start combat encounters, prompting all participants to roll for initiative. The addon automatically manages turn order with real-time synchronization across all party members.
